Who We Are

Annmarie Flanagan

A board-certified Family Nurse Practitioner and Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner. Who has been in the nursing field for over 30 years. Her main focus is on working with the individual and family to get them to their highest level of mental health. Top specialties include medication management and wellness coaching, as well as the treatment of depression, bipolar, anxiety, PTSD, OCD, ADD, and ADHD.

Services
